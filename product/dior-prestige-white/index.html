Dior Prestige White

£125.00

This UV base envelops the skin in a tailor-made protective veil with lasting wear and proven effectiveness. The incredibly light and silky texture offers a feeling of absolute comfort. Perfectly protected for 8 hours, the skin glows with a new luminosity.
The Dior expert tip: apply UV Base to the entire face. Spread the base from the center outwards and slightly upwards. This enveloping technique ensures the highest protection while smoothing the face. Complete the application by blending the texture from the chin towards the neck, and from the contours of the face towards the hairline and brow line, for a seamless finish. Apply before your makeup.
